This revised document comprehensively captures the features, workflows, and logic of the Finde Business Operating System (BOS), incorporating the specific user hierarchy and future-dated features from your source documents.

1. Core Concept: Multi-Modal Discovery Engine

Finde is a system designed to bridge the gap between physical commerce and digital transactions. It allows users to transform raw media (video, audio, or images) into searchable, transactable listings through an AI-driven "Sieve" process.

2. User Hierarchy & Access Logic

Guest Users
Definition: Individuals who interact with the system via QR codes or links without having the app.
Capabilities: They can do everything from browsing to patronizing products, services, or events.
Payments: They are allowed to make transactions through integrated systems like VISA or MOMO.
Constraints: They cannot create any content (Products, Services, Events, or Objects).

Private Users
Definition: Authenticated users who have signed up but have not provided a Ghana Card or indicated business status.
Registration: The main registrant must upload details for identification.
Capabilities: They can create Objects (O) and Events (E).
Constraints: Their content is strictly non-searchable and accessible only via direct links, QR codes, or PIN protection.
Upgrading: Posting an Event requires Ghana Card registration; listing Products or Services requires moving to a Business Account.
Profile Features: Includes a history of searches, a list of "Loved" items, items purchased, and a list of followed businesses.

Business Users (Unverified vs. Verified)
Registration: Must indicate business intent, provide a description, location (Google Maps), and years of operation.
Officially Registered (Verified/Orange Tick): Businesses validated via Ghana Card, TIN, or Business Certificate.
Assurance Logic: Unverified businesses can be assured/verified after 3 months of usage.
Capabilities: Full visibility in "Search by Image" and proximity results, full inventory management, and creation of AI Agents.
Integration: Can add social media handles to their pages which redirect users to those platforms.

3. The PSE+O Framework

Entities are organized into a hierarchy to facilitate discovery:
Products (P): Tangible items for sale, featuring inventory tracking. Can be uploaded as units or categories.

Services (S): Intangible offerings listed under a business page.
Events (E): Organizable activities with ticket payment integration. Visitors are automatically added to a "Get Notified" list when exiting the event page.
Objects (O): Atomic media units (images, audio, files) that provide context.
Logic: Can be attached to a P, S, or E via drag-and-drop or exist as a standalone QR code (e.g., a birthday cake with an embedded song).

4. Key Discovery & Creation Features

The AI Sieve: Extracts frames and metadata from uploaded video or audio to draft listings for human approval.
Importing: Products can also be imported via Excel sheets or from other social media accounts.
Search by Image: A visual engine where taking a photo (plus location) finds exact/similar products in the user's vicinity.
Notifier System: Visitors opt-in to updates; businesses can send broad notifications to these subscribers regarding new arrivals.

5. Business Operations & AI Agents

Businesses can create digital "Agents" (initially free) to handle daily functions:
Sales Agents: Understand business logic and prices to chat with users on WhatsApp, Telegram, or the Finde app.
Marketing Agents: Create campaign strategies and manage/post to linked social media platforms.
Automation Agents: Suggest and handle automation processes from end-to-end.
Business Reasoning Agents: Research and provide ways to improve the business.
Dashboards: Provide views of sales, inventory, message history, and "Notifier" subscriber counts.

6. Trust, Safety & Privacy
Oversight Console: Finde maintains "Compliance Access". An AI agent scans all uploads (public and private) for illegal activity like narcotics or weapons.


PIN Logic: Private pages or objects can be locked behind a PIN.
Payments: Integrated MOMO/Visa processing with automated invoice generation and email receipts.

7. Future Features
Web-as-a-Feature: The ability for business pages to be captured as web pages and deployed as standalone websites with custom domains.
QR Code Games: A feature where a business creates a game as an Event, attaching several clues on a trail captured under QR codes.
